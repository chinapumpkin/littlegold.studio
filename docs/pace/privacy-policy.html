Pace

Privacy Policy

Overview

Pace is developed by Little Gold Studio. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using Pace, you agree to the practices described here.

Data We Collect

Always collected (no account required):

  • Location / GPS Data: Collected only while you are actively running. Used to calculate distance, pace, and generate your route map. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Run Records: Distance, duration, pace, and GPS tracks. Stored locally in an encrypted database on your device.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics. Contains no personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g. which features are used) via Firebase Analytics. Collected only after you accept this policy during onboarding.

Collected when you sign in with Google (optional, for Pro features):

  • Google Account Identifier: Your Google account UID (not your name or email) is used to link your data to your account.
  • Synced Run History: Your run records are securely synced to Google Firebase so you can restore them on a new device.
  • AI Companion Context: Aggregated run statistics (total runs, average pace, streak) are sent to our AI service to generate personalized companion responses. Raw GPS tracks are never sent.
  • Subscription Status: Managed via Google Play and RevenueCat. We store your subscription status tied to your Google UID to restore Pro access across reinstalls.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment card details (handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Contacts, messages, photos, or other device data
  • Health data beyond what you generate by running (no heart rate, no medical data)
  • Your precise location when the app is in the background or closed

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide run tracking, distance calculation, and route display
  • To power your AI companion's real-time encouragement and personalized responses
  • To restore your run history and subscription when you reinstall the app
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand which features are used and improve the experience

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to any third party.

AI and Fitness Data

When using AI companion features, anonymized run statistics are sent to a language model to generate companion dialogue. This data does not include your identity, precise GPS coordinates, or any personally identifiable information. It is used solely to generate your companion's responses and is not used to train AI models.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Crash reporting, analytics, authentication, and cloud storage
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management and purchase validation
  • Google Play — Payment processing for subscriptions

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your device until you clear it in Settings or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud-synced data: Retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Analytics and crash data: Retained per Google's standard Firebase retention policies (typically 14 months).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated cloud data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in your device's app settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at littlegold.studio@icloud.com.

Children's Privacy

Pace is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at: littlegold.studio@icloud.com

Pace

隐私政策

概述

Pace 由 Little Gold Studio 开发。我们重视您的隐私。本政策说明我们收集哪些数据、为何收集以及如何保护这些数据。使用 Pace 即表示您同意本政策中描述的做法。

我们收集的数据

始终收集（无需账号）：

  • 位置/GPS 数据：仅在您主动跑步期间收集，用于计算距离、配速和生成路线图。仅存储在您的设备本地。
  • 跑步记录：距离、时长、配速和 GPS 轨迹，存储在设备本地加密数据库中。
  • 崩溃报告：通过 Firebase Crashlytics 收集匿名崩溃诊断数据，不含任何个人身份信息。
  • 使用分析：通过 Firebase Analytics 收集匿名使用统计（如功能使用频率）。仅在您在引导页面同意本政策后才会收集。

使用 Google 账号登录后收集（可选，用于 Pro 功能）：

  • Google 账号标识符：使用您的 Google UID（非姓名或邮箱）将数据与账号关联。
  • 同步跑步历史：跑步记录安全同步至 Google Firebase，以便在新设备上恢复数据。
  • AI 伴侣上下文：汇总的跑步统计数据（总次数、平均配速、连续天数）发送至 AI 服务以生成个性化伴侣回应。原始 GPS 轨迹不会发送。
  • 订阅状态：通过 Google Play 和 RevenueCat 管理，与您的 Google UID 关联，以便重装后恢复 Pro 权益。

我们不收集的数据

  • 您的姓名、邮箱地址或手机号码
  • 支付卡信息（完全由 Google Play 处理）
  • 通讯录、短信、照片或其他设备数据
  • 超出跑步范围的健康数据（无心率、无医疗数据）
  • 应用在后台或关闭时的精确位置

数据使用方式

  • 提供跑步追踪、距离计算和路线显示
  • 驱动 AI 伴侣的实时鼓励和个性化回应
  • 在重装应用时恢复您的跑步历史和订阅状态
  • 诊断崩溃问题，改进应用稳定性
  • 了解功能使用情况，改善用户体验

我们不将您的数据用于广告目的，不向任何第三方出售您的数据。

AI 与健身数据

使用 AI 伴侣功能时，匿名化的跑步统计数据会发送至语言模型以生成伴侣对话。这些数据不包含您的身份信息、精确 GPS 坐标或任何个人身份信息，仅用于生成伴侣回应，不用于训练 AI 模型。

第三方服务

  • Firebase（Google） — 崩溃报告、分析、身份验证和云存储
  • RevenueCat — 订阅管理和购买验证
  • Google Play — 订阅支付处理

数据保留

  • 本地数据：保留在您的设备上，直到您在设置中清除或卸载应用。
  • 云同步数据：在您的账号有效期间保留。您可随时申请删除。
  • 分析和崩溃数据：按照 Google Firebase 标准保留策略（通常为 14 个月）。

您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问：申请查看我们持有的您的数据副本
  • 更正：申请更正不准确的数据
  • 删除：申请删除您的账号及相关云端数据
  • 退出：随时在设备应用设置中关闭分析数据收集

如需行使上述权利，请联系：littlegold.studio@icloud.com

儿童隐私
