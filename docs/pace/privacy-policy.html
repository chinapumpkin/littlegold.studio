Pace

隐私政策

概述

Pace（以下简称"本应用"）由 Little Gold Studio 开发。我们重视您的隐私，本政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

我们收集的数据

  • 位置数据：本应用在跑步过程中收集 GPS 位置信息，用于计算距离、配速和生成跑步路线。位置数据仅在您主动开始跑步时才会被收集，且仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。
  • 跑步记录：跑步数据（距离、时间、配速、GPS 轨迹等）保存在您设备本地的数据库中，不会与第三方共享。
  • 崩溃与诊断数据：本应用集成了 Firebase Crashlytics，当应用崩溃时会自动收集匿名的崩溃报告，帮助我们改进稳定性。这些数据不包含个人身份信息。
  • 使用分析：本应用集成了 Firebase Analytics，收集匿名的应用使用统计信息（如功能使用频率），用于改善用户体验。

我们不收集的数据

  • 姓名、邮箱、手机号等个人身份信息
  • 支付信息
  • 通讯录、短信、照片等其他设备数据

数据存储与安全

您的跑步记录和 GPS 数据仅存储在您的设备本地，我们无法访问这些数据。Firebase 收集的匿名分析和崩溃数据由 Google 按照其隐私政策进行处理和存储。

第三方服务

  • Firebase（Google） — 崩溃报告与匿名使用分析

儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系：littlegold.studio@icloud.com

Pace

Privacy Policy

Overview

Pace is developed by Little Gold Studio. We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

  • Location Data: The app collects GPS location data during runs to calculate distance, pace, and generate route maps. Location is only collected when you actively start a run and is stored locally on your device only — never uploaded to any server.
  • Run Records: Running data (distance, time, pace, GPS tracks, etc.) is stored in a local database on your device and is never shared with third parties.
  • Crash & Diagnostic Data: The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect anonymous crash reports when the app crashes, helping us improve stability. These reports contain no personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Analytics: The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g. feature usage frequency) to help improve the user experience.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number
  • Payment information
  • Contacts, messages, photos, or other device data

Data Storage & Security

Your run records and GPS data are stored only on your device and are not accessible by us. Anonymous analytics and crash data collected by Firebase are processed and stored by Google under their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — Crash reporting and anonymous usage analytics

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: littlegold.studio@icloud.com
